# Spinny — Project Context & Brand Brief

## What is Spinny?

**Spinny** is a Canada-based, on-demand laundry and dry cleaning service platform. It connects customers who need laundry done with professional laundry vendors and independent drivers — creating a seamless pickup → clean → deliver experience through a single app.

> **One-liner:** *The first laundry app that doesn't lock you into monthly subscriptions. Pay only for what you use.*

---

## Core Product

| Aspect | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Service-based marketplace platform |
| **Market** | Canada (launching in major cities) |
| **Model** | Pay-per-use (no monthly passes, no subscriptions) |
| **Platforms** | Web app, iOS, Android |
| **Website** | washr.ca |

### How It Works

1. **Schedule a Pickup** — Customer selects a time slot and enters their address
2. **Driver Arrives** — A Washr driver picks up the laundry bag from the customer's door
3. **Expert Cleaning** — Garments are taken to a vetted local laundry vendor for professional cleaning
4. **Delivered Fresh** — Clean, folded, and packaged garments are delivered back to the customer's doorstep

---

## The Three-Sided Marketplace

Washr operates as a **three-sided platform** connecting:

### 1. Customers
- Busy professionals, families, students, and anyone who values their time
- People who want premium garment care without the hassle
- Urban Canadians in metro areas

### 2. Laundry Vendors (Partners)
- Local laundromats, dry cleaners, and professional cleaning businesses
- Washr helps them **get more orders** and **connect online** — expanding their customer base beyond walk-ins
- Vendors set their own pricing and service offerings
- Dashboard for order management, analytics, and customer reviews

### 3. Drivers
- Independent drivers who handle pickup and delivery logistics
- Flexible gig-economy work with optimized routing
- Washr provides the driver service infrastructure to vendors who don't have their own delivery capability

## Unique Selling Points (USPs)

### 🚫 No Monthly Subscriptions
> **The first laundry app that doesn't run on monthly pay passes.**
> Unlike competitors (Rinse, Hampr, etc.) that require weekly/monthly commitments, Washr is purely **pay-what-you-use**. Order once a year or five times a week — no lock-in, no minimum spend, no cancellation fees.

### 🚗 Driver-Powered Logistics
Washr provides a full driver network to local vendors, enabling even small neighborhood dry cleaners to offer pickup & delivery without hiring their own fleet.

### 🏪 Empowering Local Vendors
Rather than building mega-laundry facilities, Washr partners with existing local businesses — giving them online visibility, order flow, and delivery infrastructure they couldn't afford alone.

### 💎 Premium Garment Care
- Commercial-grade cleaning equipment
- Eco-friendly, hypoallergenic detergents
- Individual garment inspection
- Professional folding and packaging
- Stain treatment and special care options

### ⚡ Convenience-First UX
- Schedule pickups in under 30 seconds
- Real-time order tracking
- In-app communication with drivers
- Transparent pricing — no hidden fees
- Rating system for quality assurance

## Asset Generation Guidelines

When generating **videos, images, banners, social media content, or marketing assets** for Washr, use this context:

### Do's ✅
- Use the **Washr Orange (#F28C28)** as the primary accent color
- Feature **clean, folded laundry** and **happy customers**
- Show the **app interface** on modern phones when relevant
- Emphasize **"No subscription"** messaging prominently
- Include the **Washr logo** (orange "W" icon + "washr" wordmark)
- Use **Canadian imagery** — maple elements subtly, Canadian cities
- Highlight the **three-sided marketplace** (customers, vendors, drivers)
- Show **doorstep delivery** moments

### Don'ts ❌
- Don't use generic stock photos of laundry
- Don't reference subscription/monthly pricing
- Don't use competitor brand colors (Rinse gold, etc.)
- Don't show dirty/messy laundry — always clean, aspirational imagery
- Don't use overly corporate or cold visual tones
- Don't forget the warmth — Washr is friendly, not clinical

### Asset Formats Commonly Needed
- **Social Media:** Instagram (1080×1080, 1080×1920), Facebook (1200×630), Twitter/X (1600×900), LinkedIn (1200×627)
- **Web Banners:** Hero (1920×1080), CTA sections, feature cards
- **Video:** 16:9 (1920×1080) for web, 9:16 (1080×1920) for stories/reels
- **Print:** Business cards, flyers, vehicle wraps for driver fleet
- **App Store:** Screenshots (1290×2796 iPhone, 1080×1920 Android)
